【ずじまいだ】

［V-ずじまいだ］

（1）出張しゅっ ちょうで香港ほん こんへ行いったが、いそがしくて友ともだちには会あわずじまいだった。I went to Hong Kong on business, but I was so busy that I came home without seeing any friends.
（2）せっかく買かったブーツも今年ことしの冬ふゆは暖あたたかくて使つかわずじまいだった。Even though I’d gone out and bought a new pair of boots, the winter was so warm this year that I never used them once.
（3）夏休なつ やすみのまえにたくさん本ほんを借かりたが、結局けっ きょく読よまずじまいで、先生せん せいにしかられた。Although I borrowed lots of books before the summer vacation, I ended up not reading any of them and got scolded by my teacher.
（4）旅行りょ こうでお世話せ わになった人ひとたちに、お礼れいの手紙て がみを出ださずじまいではずかしい。I am ashamed of myself for not sending a letter of thanks to the people who were kind to me during the trip.
Means that something has concluded without the speaker doing a certain action. Often conveys a feeling of regret.

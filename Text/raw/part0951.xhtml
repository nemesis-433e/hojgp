【まみれ】

［Nまみれ］

（1）子供こ どもたちは汗あせまみれになっても気きにせずに遊あそんでいる。／The children keep on playing without even caring that they are dripping with sweat.
（2）あの仏像ぶつ ぞうは何年なん ねんも放ほうっておかれたので、ほこりまみれだ。／Because that Buddhist statue was neglected for years, it is covered in dust.
（3）犯行はん こう現場げん ばには血ちまみれのナイフが残のこされていた。／A knife covered in blood was left at the scene of the crime.
Shows the condition that something dirty covers the whole surface of a thing. It can be used in the forms Nまみれになる, Nまみれだ and Nまみれの and can only be used with a limited range of nouns such as sweat, dust, blood, mud, etc.

【ものか】

［Na なものか］

［A-いものか］

［V-るものか］

1. ...ものか／...もんか

（1）A:はさみも持もって行いく？／Are you taking scissors?
B:そんなもの必要ひつ ようなもんか。／Why would that be necessary?
（2）A:藤井ふじ いさんが一番いち ばんになったそうね。／They say Fujii-san has become number one.
B:そんなことがあるもんか。何なにかの間違ま ちがいだろう。／That can’t be! There must be some kind of mistake.
（3）こんな複雑ふく ざつな文章ぶん しょう、訳やくせるものですか。／There is no way such a complicated sentence can be translated.
（4）誘さそわれたって、だれが行いくものか。／Even if they invited me, there’s no way I would go.
（5）あんな人ひとに、頼たのむもんか。／There is no way I would ask such a person a favor.
（6）誰だれが人ひとに手渡て わたしたりするものですか。／There is no way I would hand this over to someone else.
With a falling intonation, expresses a feeling of strong denial. Examples (4)-(6) express the speaker’s strong intention that something will not be done. Used in casual conversation.
Usually, ものか is used by men and the polite ものですか is used by women.

2. V-ないものだろうか

（1）もう少すこし涼すずしくならないものかなあ。／I wish it would get a little cooler.
（2）もう少すこし分わかりやすく書かけなかったものか。／Couldn’t you have written it (in a way that would have been) a little easier to understand?
（3）何なんとかして晩ばんまでに青森あお もりまで行いけないものか考かんがえてみよう。／Let’s think if there is a way for us to get to Aomori by evening.
（4）だれかに協力きょう りょくしてもらえないものだろうか。／Can’t I get some cooperation from somebody on this?
（5）2時間じ かんの通勤つう きん時間じ かんを何なんとか利用り ようできないものかと考かんがえた。／I thought about whether there was some way I could take advantage of my two-hour commute.
（6）A:彼かれと話はなしができないものでしょうか。／I wonder if we could talk to him.
B:何なんとか方法ほう ほうを考かんがえましょう。／Let me think of some way to do that.
Shows that the speaker wishes for a certain event to take place. The ...なかったものか in example (2) contains a feeling of bewilderment that something has not taken place. Also, when used with the sentence ending (と)考える, means that whether or not the realization of an event or occurrence is possible is being considered. Can also be used, as in (6), to make a tentative request.

3. どうしたもの（だろう）か

（1）反対派はん たい はへの説明せつ めいはどうしたものかね。／How are we supposed to explain this to the opposition?
（2）彼かれらに対たいする報酬ほう しゅうはどうしたものだろうか。／What should I do to remunerate them?
（3）今後こん ごの資金繰し きん ぐりはどうしたものか、少すこし考かんがえさせてくれ。／Let me think a little about what we should do for funding from now on.
Expresses a feeling of confusion over what the best course of action is. Can also be used as a question of someone present.

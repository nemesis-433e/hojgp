【だにしない】

［Nだにしない］

（1）このような事故じ こが起おきるとは想像そう ぞうだにしなかった。／I never even imagined that an accident like this could occur.
（2）衛兵えい へいは直立ちょくりつ不動ふ どうのまま、微動び どうだにしない。／The sentinel stands erect and doesn’t make the slightest movement.
（3）そんな危険き けんをおかすなんて考かんがえるだに恐おそろしい。／It is terrifying just to think about taking such a risk.
（4）一顧いっ こだにしない。／He doesn’t even give it a thought.
（5）一瞥いち べつだにしない。／He doesn’t even give it a glance.
This is a literary expression that means “doesn’t even do N,” “doesn’t do N at all.”
Sometimes it may be combined with an affirmative form and used to mean “only, just,” as in the expression 思うだに恐ろしい (terrifying just to think about...).
Examples (4) and (5) are idiomatic expressions. Example (4) means “doesn’t consider...at all,” and (5) means “doesn’t look at all.”

【にかこつけて】

［Nにかこつけて］

（1）仕事し ごとにかこつけてヨーロッパ旅行りょ こうを楽たのしんできた。／I enjoyed traveling Europe under the pretext of being on a business trip.
（2）病気びょう きにかこつけて仕事し ごともせずにぶらぶらしている。／She is lazing around without working under the pretext of being sick.
（3）接待せっ たいにかこつけて上等じょう とうな酒さけを思おもいっきり飲のんできた。／I drank all the quality liquor I wanted under the pretext of entertaining a business acquaintance.
With a noun expressing a thing/state/event, expresses the meaning of “under the pretext of doing something even though it is not a direct reason or cause.”

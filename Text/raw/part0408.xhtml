【だらけ】

［Nだらけだ］

（1）間違ま ちがいだらけの答案とう あんが返かえってきた。／I got back an answer sheet full of mistakes.
（2）子供こ どもは泥どろだらけの足あしで部屋へ やに上あがってきた。／Children came into the room with mud all over their shoes.
（3）彼かれは借金しゃっ きんだらけだ。／He’s deep in debt. / He has a lot of debt.
（4）「傷きずだらけの青春せい しゅん」という映画えい がを見みた。／I watched a film called Bruised-up Youth (original title: Split Decisions).
（5）彼女かの じょの部屋へ やは本ほんだらけだ。／Her room is packed with books.
Describes a situation where there is a lot of something and only that. Unlike ...でいっぱい, indicates a negative feeling of the speaker about such a situation. (5) gives the feeling that there are not just many books in the room, there are too many, and thus the room is messy.

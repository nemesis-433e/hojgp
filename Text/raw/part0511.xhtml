【てやる】

［V-てやる］

（1）子供こ どもに新あたらしい自転車じ てん しゃを買かってやったら、翌日よく じつ盗ぬすまれてしまった。／The day after I bought my child a new bicycle, it was stolen.
（2）東京とう きょうの弟おとうとに、今年ことしもふるさとの名物めいぶつを送おくってやった。／This year again, I sent some specialties from our hometown to my younger brother in Tokyo.
（3）犬いぬを広ひろい公園こう えんで放はなしてやったら、うれしそうに走はしり回まわっていた。／After letting my dog loose in the spacious park, he was running around joyfully.
（4）A:荷物に もつ、重おもかったら持もってやるよ。／If your bag is too heavy, I’ll carry it for you.
B:あ、いい、大丈夫だい じょう ぶ。／Oh, no, I’m OK.
（5）こんな給料きゅう りょうの安やすい会社かい しゃ、いつでも辞やめてやる。／I’m ready to quit this low-paying company anytime.
（6）A:あんたなんか死しねばいいのよ。／Why don’t you just go ahead and die?
B:そんなに言いうんなら、ほんとに死しんでやる。／If that’s what you want, then I’ll kill myself just to make you happy.
Expresses an action performed by the speaker (or someone in the speaker’s in-group) for someone of lower status, or for an animal. Sometimes used as an expression of anger, as in (5) and (6), with the meaning that the speaker is ready to perform an action undesirable to the listener. When the addressee is the same status as the speaker, the form V-てあげる is used.

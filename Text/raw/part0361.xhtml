【だい】

Used by men in casual conversation.

1. 疑ぎ問もん表ひょう現げん+だい

（1）いま何時なん じだい？／What time is it now?
（2）いつだい？　花子はな この入学式にゅう がく しきは。／When is it? Hanako’s entrance ceremony, I mean.
（3）その手紙て がみだれからだい？／So who’s the letter from?
（4）どうだい。元気げん きかい。／So how are things? Are you doing well?
（5）そんなことだれから聞きいたんだい？／Who’d you hear that from?
（6）何時なん じにどこに集あつまればいいんだい？／What time and where should we meet?
（7）どうだい、すごいだろう。／Whaddaya think? Great, isn’t it!
（8）何なんだい、今頃いま ごろやってきて。もう準備じゅん びはぜんぶ終おわったよ。／Whaddaya think you’re doing, arriving at this hour? We’re already finished getting everything ready!
Attaches to interrogative expressions, such as question words or clauses that include a question word. Expresses the feeling of asking the listener a question. Sometimes used as an exclamation that conveys the feeling of asking a question or criticizing, as in (7) and (8). A slightly old-fashioned expression used in spoken language. Usually used by adult males.

2. ...だい。

［N／Na　だい］

（1）そんなことうそだい。／That’s a lie!
（2）いやだい。絶対ぜっ たい教おしえてあげないよ。／No. I won’t tell you, ever.
（3）ぼくのはこれじゃないよ。それがぼくのだい。／This one isn’t mine. That one’s mine.
Used by boys to make a strong assertion.

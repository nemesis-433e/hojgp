【かろう】

［N／Na　ではなかろう］

［A-かろう］

［A-く（は）なかろう］

（1）その話はなしは真実しん じつではなかろう。／That is probably not true.
（2）親おやをなくしてはさぞや辛つらかろう。／He’s probably having a hard time after losing his one of his parents.
（3）少すこしは苦くるしむのもよかろう。／It is probably good for him to suffer a little.
（4）手術しゅ じゅつはさほどむずかしくはなかろうと存ぞんじます。／We believe the operation will probably not be that difficult.
Attaches to i-adjectives or ではな, which is the negative form of だ without い. Expresses supposition, and has almost the same meaning as だろう. The suppositional usage of V-よう corresponds to the form かろうfor verbs. A literary and old-fashioned expression used in written language or formal spoken language. In everyday spoken language, だろう is used.

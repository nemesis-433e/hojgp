【じゃないだろうか】

［N／Na　（なん）じゃないだろうか］

［A／V　んじゃないだろうか］

（1）もう帰かえってしまったんじゃないだろうか。／She hasn’t already gone back home, has she?
（2）あいつはやる気きがないんじゃないだろうか。／It’s not that he isn’t very motivated, is it?
This is the spoken language form of ではないだろうか. The polite form is (ん)じゃないでしょうか. When the speaker uses this expression to talk to him/herself, it expresses a supposition. On the other hand, in conversation, it often means that the speaker is trying to confirm his/her supposition with the listener.

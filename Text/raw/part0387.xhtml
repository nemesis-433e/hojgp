【だって3】

（1）A:どうして外そとで遊あそばないの。／Why aren’t you playing outside?
B:だって寒さむいんだもん。／Because it’s cold.
（2）A:にんじん、残のこさずにちゃんと食たべなさい。／Eat all your carrots and don’t leave any on your plate.
B:だってきらいだもん。／But I don’t like them!
（3）A:夕刊ゆう かんまだかな。／I wonder if the evening paper has come yet.
B:だって、今日きょうは日曜日にち よう びでしょ、来こないわよ。／Well, today is Sunday, so we’re not going to get a paper.
（4）A:きのうはどうして待まってくれなかったの。／Why didn’t you wait for me yesterday?
B:だってあそこの喫茶店きっ さ てん、人ひとが多おおくて居いづらかったんだよ。／Because that café was so crowded I felt uncomfortable staying there.
Used by the speaker to reply when asked the reason for something and means “it is because....” Can be used even when the reason is not asked explicitly, as in (2) and (3). Children, especially, use it in the form だって...もの/もん when they talk back. Adults sometimes use it, too. Informal spoken language.

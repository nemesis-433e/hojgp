【をかぎりに】

［Nをかぎりに］

（1）今日きょうをかぎりに今いままでのことはきれいさっぱり忘わすれよう。／As of today, let’s forget everything that has happened until now.
（2）明日あ すの大おお晦日みそかをかぎりにこの店みせは閉店へい てんする。／As of tomorrow on New Year’s Eve, this store will close.
（3）この会かいは今回こん かいをかぎりに解散かい さんすることとなりました。／It is decided that this meeting is dissolved as of now.
（4）みんなは声こえを限かぎりに叫さけんだが、何なんの返事へん じも返かえってこなかった。／Everyone shouted at the top of their voices but they got no reply.
Attaches to words demonstrating time, such as “today” and “this time,” and means “with that time as the last.” Often used with words which include the time of the utterance. Example (4) is an idiomatic expression meaning “at the top of one’s voice.”

【ぬく】

［R-ぬく］

（1）苦くるしかったが最後さい ごまで走はしりぬいた。／It was very hard, but I ran all the way to the end.
（2）一度いち ど始はじめたからには、あきらめずに最後さい ごまでやりぬこう。／Now that we’ve started, let’s see it through to the end, without giving up.
（3）考かんがえ抜ぬいた結果けっ かの決心けっ しんだからもう変かわることはない。／I reached this decision after thinking long and hard about it, so I’m not going to change my mind.
（4）この長ながい漂流ひょう りゅうを耐たえ抜ぬくことができたのは、「ここで死しにたくない」という強つよい気持き もちがあったからだと思おもいます。／I think the reason I was able to survive being adrift at sea for so long is that I had a strong feeling that “I don’t want to die here.”
Means finishing all of the necessary actions or processes, right up to the end. Conveys a strong implication that suffering has been endured in order to accomplish something.

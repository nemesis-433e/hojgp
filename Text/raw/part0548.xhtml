【とうてい...ない】

（1）うちの息子むすこの実力じつ りょくでは、東大とう だい合格ごう かくはとうてい無理む りだ。／With our son's academic ability, getting into Tokyo University is utterly impossible.
（2）彼女かの じょが僕ぼくを裏切うら ぎるなんてとうていあり得えない。／It is simply unthinkable that she would betray me.
（3）歴史れき しの長ながさにおいて、日本に ほんの大学だい がくは西洋せい ようの古ふるい大学だい がくにはとうてい及およばない。／When it comes to the length of their histories, the universities of Japan are far behind the old established institutions of Europe.
Expresses that no matter what method is used or how a person thinks about something, that thing is unattainable, impossible, or inconceivable. Has the tone of written language.

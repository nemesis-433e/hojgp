【ふしがある】

（1）彼かれはどうも行いくのをいやがっているふしがある。／It seems as though he’s got something that makes him reluctant to go.
（2）犯人はん にんは、その日ひ被害者ひ がい しゃが家いえにいることを知しっていたと思おもわれるふしがある。／It would seem that the culprit knew the victim would be at home that day.
（3）その男おとこの言動げん どうには、どことなくあやしいふしがある。／There’s something suspicious about that man’s words and actions.
Means “it would seem to be....” Used when it is presumed to be the case judging from someone’s words or actions, as in (1) and (2). Can also be used when “there are reasons to be suspicious,” as in the case of (3).

【じゃない】

［N／Na　じゃない］

（1）A:雨あめ？／Rain?
B:いや、雨あめじゃない。／No, not rain.
（2）A:雨あめじゃない？／Isn’t it raining?
B:ええ、雨あめよ。／Yes, it’s raining.
（3）あら、雨あめじゃない。せんたく物ものいれなくちゃ。／Wow, look at the rain! Gotta bring in the laundry!
Casual way of saying ではない. Example (1) is a negative sentence, and the な is pronounced with a higher tone. Example (2) is a negative question with rising intonation. Example (3) is not negation but assertion, and the whole expression じゃない has falling intonation. Spoken language. Used by both men and women.
